About Us

Proper Child International School was established in 2011 with a vision to provide quality education that brings out the best in every child. Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the future.

Located at Proper Child Avenue, off Palm View Road, Umuotube Chokota, Igbo Etche, Rivers State, our school provides a conducive learning environment with modern facilities.

Nigerian National Anthem (2025)

Nigeria, we hail thee,

Our own dear native land,

Though tribe and tongue may differ,

In brotherhood, we stand,

Nigerians all, and proud to serve

Our sovereign Motherland.


Our flag shall be a symbol

That truth and justice reign,

In peace or battle honour'd,

And this we count as gain,

To hand on to our children

A banner without stain.


O God of all creation,

Grant this our one request,

Help us to build a nation

Where no man is oppressed,

And so with peace and plenty

Nigeria may be blessed.

To be faithful, loyal and honest

To serve Nigeria with all my strength

To defend her unity

And uphold her honor and glory
